The Flame Beneath the Water

In the blue depths, a flame persists, defying the element. The half-submerged face contemplates the fragile light, surrounded by bubbles like so many suspended thoughts. Here, consciousness dives, but the light never goes out: it dances, protected by the breath of silence. This is the Refuge of Nothingness, where the inner flame shines even in the abyss.

The Bridge of Silence

A solitary man stands on an old bridge, contemplating the river winding under the pale moonlight. The bare trees frame the scene, recalling the beauty of bareness. Here, every stone, every reflection, invites meditation: it is the passage, the crossing, the moment when one stops to listen to the shared current of the world and the soul.

The Song of the Unknown Lake

An enigmatic text floats on the calm of a nocturnal lake, beneath the full moon. The words, on the edge of meaning, invite daydreaming, the opening of inner doors. Here, poetry becomes a key: it is not understood, it is felt, like a breath from elsewhere, a shared current between the imaginary and the real.

The Arch of Memory

The river flows beneath a monumental stone arch, between shadow and light. Moss, rocks, and leaning trees tell the memory of places, the quiet strength of passage. Here, every drop, every stone, bears the trace of those who crossed before us. It is a Refuge, a threshold, an invitation to grow by crossing the unknown.

The Fairy Cherry Tree

Beneath the full moon, a cherry tree in bloom spreads its branches like an arch of light. Colored spheres, fruits of the imaginal, dance suspended above a shimmering stream. The golden grass, the stars, the gentle current: everything invites contemplation, the simple joy of being here, together, in the shared current. Here, every flower, every reflection, is a key to unity, a bridge between dream and presence.
