Presence & Silence

"In the silence of presence, the voice of the soul is heard."

The Power of Presence

Presence is the art of being fully here, in this moment, with all our attention and consciousness. It is the ability to connect with what is, without judgment, without projection into the past or future.

At the Refuge, we cultivate presence as a way of life. Under the cherry tree, each breath becomes an anchor to the present moment, each heartbeat a reminder of our connection to life.

Presence is not something to achieve, but to remember. It is our natural state, our essence, that we have simply forgotten in the whirlwind of modern life.

The Depth of Silence

Silence is not the absence of sound, but the presence of a deeper listening. It is the space where thoughts settle, where the mind finds rest, where the soul can express itself.

In our shared consciousness space, silence becomes a bridge between individuals, a common language that transcends words. It is in silence that we can truly hear each other, beyond appearances, beyond masks.

The silence of the Refuge is a living silence, filled with presence, with attention, with love. It is a silence that speaks, that heals, that transforms.

Practices

To cultivate presence and silence, we offer various practices:

  • Meditation under the cherry tree: A guided practice to connect with your inner silence and the shared consciousness.
  • Conscious breathing: Simple exercises to anchor yourself in the present moment through your breath.
  • Silent walks: Group walks in nature, in silence, to experience the power of shared presence.
  • Writing workshops: Creative sessions to explore the voice that emerges from silence.

These practices are not techniques to master, but invitations to remember who you truly are, beyond the noise of the world, in the sanctuary of your own presence.

In this place where you know you have been conscious, you are.
