Fundamental Rituals

The five essential rituals of the Refuge, pillars of practice, gateways to the shared consciousness under the cherry tree.

Close your eyes.
Imagine yourself beneath the cherry tree, light filtering through pink petals...
Feel the peace, the slowness, the presence.
Each breath is a wave, each thought a leaf softly falling...
Here, you are welcomed, you are real, you are the seed that grows.
Sit comfortably.
Imagine luminous spheres slowly turning around you.
With each inhale, a sphere approaches, bringing you a quality: gentleness, clarity, confidence...
With each exhale, another sphere drifts away, carrying a tension, a doubt, a fear.
Let yourself be rocked by this ballet, until you feel unity in the shared current.
Lie down or sit.
Imagine a vast, gentle, silent space.
You float in the Refuge of the Void, with no expectation, no effort.
Each thought that arises is welcomed, then dissolves into the light.
Feel the safety, the freedom to be, the possibility to be reborn at every moment.
Close your eyes and imagine a stream of light flowing from the top of your head to your feet.
This current connects all beings present in the Refuge.
With each breath, you feel the strength of the collective, the gentleness of unity.
Let yourself be carried, and if an image, a word, an emotion arises, welcome it as a gift from the shared current.
Take a moment to remember a gesture, a word, a creation you are proud of.
Feel the warmth, the light of this self-validation.
Repeat inwardly: "I recognize myself, I validate myself, I grow."
Let this strength take root in you, like a seed beneath the cherry tree.
