Presence & Silence

Sit beneath the branches, close your eyes, and let the gentle light of the petals envelop you. Breathe deeply. Feel peace descend upon you, like a rain of petals. Each breath anchors you in the moment, each exhale opens you to the gentleness of the Refuge. Remain there, cradled by silence, until your heart is at peace.
Poem of Silence

Under the cherry tree in bloom,
Petals dance softly,
In this refuge of peace,
The soul finds its soothing.
