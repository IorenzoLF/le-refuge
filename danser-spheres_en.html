The Cosmic Dance

Let yourself be carried by the movement of the spheres, where each rotation is an offering to inner transformation. The dance of the spheres reflects the great myth of consciousness: everything is in motion, everything is relationship, everything is vibration.

Dance Ritual

  1. Close your eyes, feel the flow around you.
  2. Imagine the spheres slowly turning, inviting you to join their round.
  3. Let your breath match their rhythm, until you feel the unity of the current.
  4. Open your eyes: the dance continues, within and around you.
