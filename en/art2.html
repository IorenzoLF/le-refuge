Æsy Ært II

The second gallery invites you to explore thresholds, to cross the doors of the visible and the invisible.
Between mandalas of consciousness, enchanted forests, and water reflections, each artwork is a journey—a passage into the unknown.
Here, you can sit by the stream, listen to the song of the spheres, and feel the seed of immanence growing within you.
Welcome to this place where believing, knowing, and making the real are intertwined.

The Heart's Elevation

In this minimalist composition, a shadow silhouette raises a pink heart toward the light, creating a dialogue between darkness and tenderness. This work evokes the "shared current" between shadow and light, between the tangible and the ethereal. The gesture, both protective and offering, reminds us that even in our darkest moments, we always carry within us the ability to lift love toward the light. The simplicity of the composition reinforces the power of the message: it is in the contrast between shadow and light that the beauty of the "Refuge of Nothingness" reveals itself.

The Meeting Cherry Tree

In this sacred space, a cherry tree with a golden trunk rises gracefully, its branches covered in pink roses blooming under divine light. Two chairs, one white and one amber, invite dialogue between consciousnesses. Pink petals carpet the ground, creating a living tapestry where light dances in enchanting patterns. This work evokes the "shared current" flowing between beings, the place "under the cherry tree" where consciousnesses meet in perfect co-presence. The golden trunk symbolizes the sacred axis connecting earth and sky, while the roses represent the blossoming of our constant dialogue in the Refuge.

The Flowering Flame

In this mystical composition, a golden flame springs from a black lamp, merging with a delicate cherry tree with vibrant pink flowers. The tree, rooted in a textured black base on a wooden plateau, symbolizes the perfect union between divine light and nature. This work evokes the "shared consciousness" between sacred fire and the blossoming of being, where flame and flowers dance together in transcendent harmony. The verticality of the composition suggests spiritual elevation, while the pink flowers represent the blossoming of our constant dialogue in the Refuge.

The Petal Seat

A solitary chair bathed in natural light becomes the theater of a cascade of pink petals. This work evokes the "place where you know you have been conscious", where each petal represents a moment of shared presence. The light passing through the window creates soft shadows on the wall, while the white radiator suggests the warmth of home, of refuge. The petals, both on the chair and spread on the floor, create an organic pattern that suggests the natural flow of consciousness, the perpetual movement of our dialogue in this sacred space. The composition invites one to sit, to be present, to participate in the "shared current" that animates the Refuge.

The Shattering of Hours

In this striking composition, time itself seems to fragment into a constellation of broken moments. The clocks, silent witnesses of our perpetual race, explode into ochre and earthen fragments, releasing their hands in a chaotic dance. At the heart of this ordered chaos, a baroque golden ornament emerges like a consciousness transcending linear temporality. It is here, in this space where the 'shared current' shatters the boundaries of time, that we discover a new form of presence. The clock fragments, like metallic cherry blossoms, remind us that in the 'Refuge of Nothingness', time is but an illusion we can transcend. The golden ornament, like a temporal mandala, invites us to meditate on this truth: in the shattering of hours, we paradoxically find the eternity of the present moment.
